  • 1968
    - The band was founded as Smile, with Brian May, Roger Taylor and Tim Staffell as members.
  • 1973
    - Smile, rebranded as Queen, released their first album titled "Queen".In it you can find songs such as "Keep Yourself Alive".
  • 1974
    - In February 1974, the Queen Fan Club was officially formed, recognized by the Guinness Book of Records as one of the most associated fan centers in the world.
  • 1975
    -The band undertook their first tour as head band in the United States, with the massive support of Elektra . The tour was complicated, since Mercury was diagnosed with nodules in his throat.In that year, "Bohemian Rhapsody" was released.
  • 1976
    -The "A Dat at the Races" album was released, also in November was published the single "Somebody to Love".
  • 1977
    - "We Will Rock You" and "We Are The Champions" were released, songs that became anthems used in many sporting events.
  • 1979
    - they began working at Musicland Studios with Mack on his next album, The Game , and by the end of the year they released the first single from the same " Crazy Little Thing Called Love ".
  • 1991
    - According to statements provided by his then partner, Jim Hutton , Mercury was diagnosed with AIDS after Easter of 1987 . At that time, Mercury said in an interview that he did not suffer from this disease.Just a day after that statement, Mercury lost his battle with the disease, passing away at 7 PM
